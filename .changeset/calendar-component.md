New `Calendar` component from the Alta Vibración "Calendar" design — a booking date picker built on react-day-picker (new dependency), Spanish/Monday-first by default. Selection uses the semantic selection roles; bookable days are exposed as an `available` modifier styled with new highlight roles (`--color-highlight` orange-50 / `--color-highlight-foreground` orange-700, 5.43:1 AA).
